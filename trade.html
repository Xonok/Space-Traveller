Planets and starbases:
Min and max population are determined by planet/starbase type.
Population increases when primary industries are well-supplied and otherwise decreases.
Liquor and medicine increase population growth.
Stations:
Gain minimum and maximum population from equipped habitation modules.
Population increases when equipped machines work or when drones gather resources. Otherwise it decreases.
For both planets and stations, population growth is faster than loss.
Both stations and planets "tick" once per 3 hours, which is when they produce and consume resources.
