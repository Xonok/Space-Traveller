Traveller started as a clone of the game called Pardus, but turned into its own beast before we even got it playable.
We were also inspired by many other games and media, including:
*An obscure MMO called Star Sonata, which used to be good.
*Doctor Who
*Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy
*Factorio

Our game is meant as a place to chill and have some fun, but also to sometimes dig deep and find real complexity.
Unlike most games in space, Traveller is warm and friendly.
This game is made by a small team of volunteers. Some do more, some do less.
Most of us are from Estonia. This page lists everyone and what they did to make the game real.
